# Product Requirements Document
**wasamchaudhry.com**

**Project Name:** wasamchaudhry.com  
**Project Type:** Luxury Personal Brand Website  
**Client:** Wasam Chaudhry  
**Document Version:** 1.0

## 1. Project Overview

wasamchaudhry.com will be developed as a luxury, minimal, and professional personal brand website for Wasam Chaudhry.

The website will position Wasam as an international personal brand with a strong presence across media, podcasting, business ventures, automotive culture, global networking, and high-level collaborations.

The core focus of the website is **Wasam Chaudhry himself**. His companies, interviews, vehicles, and international network will support his personal brand, but they will not overpower it.

The website should feel **premium, clean, cinematic, confident, and quietly high-status**.

## 2. Project Goals

The website should achieve the following goals:

1. Build a strong personal brand presence for Wasam Chaudhry.
2. Establish credibility in media and podcast-related spaces.
3. Highlight Wasam’s international networking profile.
4. Present his business ventures in a refined and balanced way.
5. Encourage partnership opportunities.
6. Encourage media opportunities.
7. Allow visitors to watch or access interview and podcast content.
8. Provide links to Autolinx and related company websites.
9. Create a premium first impression through luxury design and strong visuals.
10. Keep the user experience fast, smooth, responsive, and professional.

## 3. Target Audience

The website should be designed for the following audience groups:

1. Media companies
2. Automotive clients
3. Podcast and interview audiences
4. Investors and collaborators
5. International connections
6. Global collaborators

The website should speak first to: **Media contacts and global collaborators**.

The content, design, and layout should feel relevant to people who are looking for a credible, well-connected, internationally active personal brand.

## 4. Brand Positioning

The website should position Wasam Chaudhry as:

**Entrepreneur • Executive Producer • Automotive Specialist • Connector**

The word **Connector** is important because it communicates Wasam’s ability to build networks, relationships, and opportunities across industries and countries.

The overall brand should feel:

- Luxury
- Minimal
- International
- Confident
- Personal
- Cinematic
- Business-focused
- Media-friendly
- High-status without being loud

## 5. Website Style Direction

The visual direction should follow a premium luxury style inspired by:

- Clean structure
- Premium photography
- Minimal luxury
- Rolls-Royce luxury
- Tom Ford-style minimal elegance

The design should feel polished and understated, not crowded or overly decorative.

**Approved Visual Style:**
- Matte black background
- Off-white typography
- Subtle champagne gold accents
- Dark charcoal sections
- Some light sections for contrast
- Large cinematic photography
- Clean layout with strong spacing
- Simple luxury typography
- Minimal visual clutter
- Premium editorial-style presentation
